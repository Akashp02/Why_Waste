About Us

Our project bridges the gap between hunger and excess food. The way our project functions is simple. We aim to redistribute surplus food to those who are in need of it. If you have excess food from an event, party, wedding etc, please visit our site, within some clicks your request of giving meal or take away will be sent to the individuals and restaurants/NGos. The ones who are in need or need in their localities will contact and the delivery of food will be done on mutual understanding.
